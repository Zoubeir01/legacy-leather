Welcome to Legacy Leather

Rooted in tradition, powered by craftsmanship. We deliver the finest semi-finished and vegetable-tanned hides from Tunisia to the world.

At Legacy Leather, we blend old-world mastery with modern expectations. Our hides don’t just look good, they carry the warmth of heritage, the weight of skill, and the quiet confidence of materials that were made to last. When you work with us, you’re not just sourcing leather, you’re making a statement. We source all our hides from free-range lambs, sheep, and goats raised in open pastures because quality begins with how animals are treated.

We proudly offer eco-friendly vegetable-tanned leathers that are free of harmful chemicals and made using time-honored natural processes. Sourced from the finest hides, our eco line blends durability, beauty, and environmental responsibility.

Crafted Through Generations

Legacy Leather isn’t just a brand. It’s a bond between generations, a bridge between continents, and a commitment to craftsmanship that runs deep in our blood.

Our story begins in the 1980s, in the heart of Tunisia, where my uncle founded a small tannery built on hard work, tradition, and pride in every hide produced. Over the years, that tannery became known for its premium lamb, goat, and sheepskins, semi-finished and finished with care, skill, and experience you can’t fake.

From my home base in the U.S., I founded Legacy Leather to bring our family’s work to designers, makers, and businesses who appreciate real materials with real stories behind them.
